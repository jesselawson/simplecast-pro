100% Free & Open Source.

Built for WordPress podcasters by WordPress podcasters. Simplecast Pro is 100% free, but a cup of coffee or even becoming a patron helps fund future development and support.

Integration and sharing tools.

Use simple shortcodes to share episodes on your site. As Simplecast continues to update their API, future functionality includes: create and manage new episodes straight from your dashboard!

Fast & seamlessly integrated with WordPress.

The Simplecast Pro plugin utilizes Simplecast's state-of-the-art API for rapid integration. It's tiny, fast, and makes managing your Simplecast-hosted podcasts from your WordPress admin dashboard fun.
